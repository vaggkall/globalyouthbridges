About Us

Given the challenges posed by climate change and urbanization, young individuals, especially those living in rental apartments, often face barriers in engaging with nature and adopting sustainable practices. This project aims to provide practical, small-scale solutions to help youth grow plants indoors or on balconies through portable greenhouses, thus transforming limited urban spaces into productive green areas.
