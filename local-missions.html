Local Missions

Solidarity missions are initiatives designed and carried out by young people to inspire and engage others, encouraging a mindset of solidarity that becomes ingrained in the way they think and live. With the support of EU funding, this project will bring our vision to life, launching our first three missions focused on inequality, well-being, and inclusion in Copenhagen and generally in Denmark, specifically targeting individuals with fewer opportunities who have faced significant hardships. To build a welfare state that offers opportunities, security, and a humane environment, we must start by activating the youth.

The project's objectives include raising awareness and engaging youth around social issues such as poverty, homelessness, and the challenges faced by children in need. Additionally, the project aims to promote a culture of solidarity among younger generations, while improving the living conditions of immigrants in Denmark by addressing issues of inequality, well-being, and inclusion.
